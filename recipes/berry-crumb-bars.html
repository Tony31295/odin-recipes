Berry Crumb Bars

Recipe found on allrecipes.com

Easy way to make berry bars. You can substitue with whatever berries you prefer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 4 cups raspberries
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons cornstarch
